About PicardSpace

PicardSpace is a sample application for BaseSpace that calculates sequencing metrics with the open-source tool Picard.

Analysis Failures

If your free analysis failed, please try it again by launching again from BaseSpace.

If your paid analysis failed, please contact Illumina technical support for assistance at TechSupport@illumina.com.
