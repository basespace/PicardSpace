About PicardSpace

PicardSpace is a sample application for BaseSpace that calculates sequencing metrics with the open-source tool Picard.

Example Data

To test-drive PicardSpace for free, try analyzing a sample in the BaseSpace Demo Project.

Analysis Failures

Sometime it happens -- your analysis fails. We apologize if this happens.

If your free analysis failed, please try it again by launching from BaseSpace.

If your paid analysis failed, we attempt to automatically refund your purchase. However, sometimes this isn't possible. If your analysis doesn't show 'Purchase Refunded' in the notes section of the view results screen, then please contact Illumina technical support for assistance at TechSupport@illumina.com.
